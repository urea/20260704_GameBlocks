GameBlocks Experiments

Browser-based 3D game prototypes built with GameBlocks, Three.js, Vite, and Rapier3D.

Sky Courier Fly a GameBlocks aircraft through a ring course over an island terrain. Fantasy Advance Read a hidden 3x3 defense line, break cards, and score with baseball-style bases. Contraption Lab Place fans, ramps, walls, and bumpers to guide a marble into the goal. Relay Blocks Build a snapped relay route and send a glowing signal across the board. Snake Garden Grow a 3D snake through a board using GameBlocks snake motion and play state. Circuit Dash Drive an arcade car around a GameBlocks race track with checkpoint lap logic. Hover Salvage Pilot a six-axis hover drone and recover radar-marked pickup cargo. Turret Range Aim a combat turret and clear moving targets with GameBlocks projectiles.
